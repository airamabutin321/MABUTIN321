Zanzibar Gem

"The Zanzibar Gem, with its glossy green foliage, is a low-maintenance indoor plant perfect for beginners."

Price:$20

Appearance:its shiny, dark green, waxy leaves that grow on thick, fleshy stems. These leaves are typically oval-shaped and arranged in a pinnately compound manner. The plant has a compact, slow-growing nature, reaching 2-4 feet in height and width. Its glossy foliage often gives the appearance of being artificial.
